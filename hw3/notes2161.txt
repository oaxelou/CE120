SELECITON SORT: 
Exei poluplokotita best & worst case: O(n^2).

INSERTION SORT:
Exei poluplokotita:
	worst case: O(n^2)
		Auti i periptwsi einai otan o pinakas einai anapoda taksinomimenos.
	best case: O(n)
		Auti i periptwsi einai otan einai taksinomimenos opws theloume.
		

Se auti tin periptwsi epeidi o pinakas twn onomatwn kai sta 4 arxeia eisodou den einai taksinomimenos o algorithmos tou insertion sort exei poluplokotita (< best case) kai (>worst case) , enw tou selection sort exei peripou O(n^2) kathws se kathe epanalipsi diatrexei ton pinaka olo (apo to wall mexri to teleutaio) gia na vrei to min.
Logw autou uparxoun apokliseis: to insertion sort na kanei ligoteres sugkriseis apo oti
to selection sort.
